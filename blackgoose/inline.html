This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique calledsous vide,. In sous vide cooking,you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to.In his book, cooking for geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as "ultra-low-temperature poaching."
