Tapenade (Olive spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a nig hit at parties. my father recommends:

"Make this thd night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temerature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm"

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sunried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamatao olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible.
  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated,but still retain some texture.
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
